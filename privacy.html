Important: This privacy policy applies to the Chrome extension "Blank.app" developed by Blank.app Team.

1. Data Collection

The Blank.app extension respects your privacy and does not collect any personal data:

  • No user account is required to use the extension
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • No tracking or behavior analysis is performed
  • No advertising is displayed

2. Storage

Your notes and preferences are stored locally and may sync via your Google account:

  • Uses Chrome storage APIs (chrome.storage and chrome.storage.sync)
  • Data remains on your devices and Google Sync; not shared with third parties
  • unlimitedStorage may be requested to prevent truncation of longer notes
  • You can delete your data by uninstalling the extension or clearing Chrome storage

3. Optional Location

If the user enables the Weather panel, the extension may use coarse device location or a user-provided location to fetch local weather. Location data is not persisted by the extension and is not used for profiling or advertising.
